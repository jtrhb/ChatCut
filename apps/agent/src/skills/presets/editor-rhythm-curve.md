# Rhythm Curve Editing

## Rules
- Build energy gradually over the first 30% of a clip
- Peak edit density in the middle third
- Ease out with longer holds in the final 20%
- Match cut frequency to emotional intensity
- Use J-cuts and L-cuts to maintain narrative flow
